MAMIKO USUDA

Bio

Mamiko began studying modern dance at the age of 3 under Yuka Hirata.
After graduating high school, she moved to the U.S. and attended Point Park University to obtain her Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree, with a concentration in Contemporary Dance and Choreography.

As a student, she spent her summmers touring under Iratxe Ansa as a soloist with Metamorphosis Dance. After graduation, Mamiko traveled to Europe to study "GAGA" under Emanuel Gat, Sita Ostheimer, and Spenser Theberge.

Mamiko currenly works as a freelancer - always challengeing herself in pursuit of amazing opportunities. Since returning to Japan she has performed in works by Kaiji Moriyama, Karl Alfred Schreiner, Paul Julius, Yoshimasa Ishibashi, Shinji Nakamura and others.
