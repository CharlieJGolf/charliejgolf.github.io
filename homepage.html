Mamiko Usuda

Film Works Social Media

An accomplished Contemporary Dancer, with a passion for Performance Art Photography

Bio:

Mamiko began dancing at the age of 3 studying under Yuka Hirata.
She traveled to the United States after High School to major in Dance and Choreography at Point Park University.

Mamiko has successfully established herself as a highly-talented, ambitious and well-connected freelance contemporary dancer.

She challenges herself, in pursuit of amazing opportunities, and looks forward to the honor of
performing in the opening ceremony of Tokyo’s upcoming 2020 Paralympics.

Mamiko has performed in:

  • United States
  • Mexico
  • Italy
  • Spain
  • Japan
